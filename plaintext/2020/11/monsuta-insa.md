monsuta li lon  
insa pi jan ale

jan ale li wile wawa  
tan nasin mani  
sina jo e mani mute  
la sina suli

mani li lon  
la jan mute li anpa  
li ken ala kama wawa

nasin mani li pakala  
jan ale li sama taso  
jan mute li ken ala moku  
kon moli li awen lon ma ale

monsuta li mani  
tenpo pi mani ala la  
pona taso li lon  
insa pi jan ale
